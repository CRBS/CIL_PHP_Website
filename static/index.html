Knowledge Space

About

KnowledgeSpace (KS) is a community-based encyclopedia for neuroscience, which will be supported by a structured lexicon, data, and models. KS will allow neuroscience community contributions and it will support data curation and annotation, and text mining.

KS is the result of recommendations from a community workshop held by the INCF Program on Ontologies of Neural Structures in 2012 and include the report attached below to community workshop.

2012 INCF Workshop Report

KS will be built on a semantic media platform with initial content coming from the Neuroscience Lexicon (NeuroLex), and the Brain Architecture Management System (BAMS).

KS is envisioned to be a part of a system of 3 spaces, together with the INCF Data Space and the HBP LabSpace, where neuroscientists can store, curate/annotate, and share their data.

The current status of KS is in development, with a beta-version launch scheduled for Spring 2016.
